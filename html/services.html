Services Offered
We offer 3 tiers of Servies: Bronze, Silver, and Gold upon arrival to the planet of your choice.
Gold Tier:

As part of the gold tier package apart from your travel you will receive complementary stay and travel arrangements on the planet for a week after your arrival, futhermore you will be provided with a local guide to accompany you for your first week on the planet, showing you around the planet and helping you settle in as soon as possible.

Silver Tier:

As part of the silver tier package, following your arrival on the planet you will be provided with stay and travel arrangements complementaryfor the first week. This is to help ensure a smooth transition from your life on earth to life at the new planet.

Bronze Tier:

As part of the bronze tier package, we assure you safe transport from earth to the destination planet. However on arrival to the planet, no extra facilities will be provided to you.
